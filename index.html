About Me

Hello! My name is Tyler Mills, and I have been dabbling in computer programming for as long as I've had a computer. I started out creating with simple HTML and CSS websites for video games I played in my youth, and evolved to designing automation scripts in basic languages like AutoIt.

After getting my undergraduate degree in business, I decided to attend law school. In my third year of lawschool I stumbled across Python and was instantly enamoured by the ability to quickly develop programs for automation, and interacting with remote systems. During this time I started a business that worked in the leased server space, provisions servers for clients all over the world. I built custom Python scripts to trigger overall setup and specific program installations on clients machines using XML-RPC.

Contact me via LinkedIn!

After moving on from the server provisioning business, I decided to get back into web development, and stepped into a Web Desing/Administrator role a small real estate start up to develop a MLS-like front end using a LAMP stack, as well as Python for daily automation on the back end, which fed reporting and client facing statists on properties.

Post real-estate startup, I took a role with a large financial services provider as a Data Analyst, progressing to a Data Manager role, overseeing a team of data analysts responsible for maintaining two of the largest datasets on the product.

Now, after gaining important skills in data analysis, as well as people management and strategic planning, I have deciced to pursue my passion in programming as a full-stack developer using the MERN stack.

Follow along on my journey to become a full-stack developer on GitHub: tmills-2012
